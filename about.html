About me

I'm currently working on Galactic Diner full-time. (Please consider wishlisting on Steam if you're interested!)

My favorite kind of work is looking to "surprise and delight" in a game — from intricate gameplay systems that create depth, to little aesthetic touches that people might hardly notice. I've played games basically all my life, so I've tended more detail-oriented over time.

I also enjoy teaching, cooking, running, watching anime and movies, trying to learn to draw, and trying new restaurants. I used to play the violin and still try to pick it up occasionally. I used to organize puzzle hunts with my friends, including leading development on a puzzle MMO.

My favorite game is Persona 5 Royal.

Catch me on Twitter if you want to say hi!
