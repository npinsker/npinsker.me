About me

Hi! I'm Nathan. I'm currently working on Galactic Diner full-time.

My favorite kind of work is looking to "surprise and delight" in a game — from intricate gameplay systems that create depth, to little aesthetic touches that people might hardly notice.

I also enjoy teaching, cooking, running, watching anime and movies, trying to learn to draw, and trying new restaurants. I used to play the violin and still try to pick it up occasionally. I also organize puzzle hunts with my friends, though I'm less active with that these days.
